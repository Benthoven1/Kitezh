Castles

Castles are not just tourist attractions: they are socially valuable sites for regular cultural engagement. Mulvium will house polymaths—those who explore the world through many disciplines in order to benefit humanity. The castles will implement a modular design, allowing for diverse programming: political forums, theater, educational summits, and other content for the masses, both physically and digitally.
